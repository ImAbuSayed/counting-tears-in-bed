[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I watch the moonlight shining on the floor
Always wanting you a little more
Counting tears in bed until you're near
Craving the sweet whisper I need to hear

[Verse 1]
The midnight breeze is blowing through the screen
Painting a quiet and romantic scene
I trace the empty space right by my side
With nowhere for these heavy thoughts to hide

[Pre-Chorus]
Every second feels like pouring rain
A sweet and slow, delicious kind of pain
My hands remember how to trace your skin
I'm ready for the magic to begin

[Chorus]
Oh, counting tears in bed without your touch
I never knew I'd want someone this much
Your memory is a fire in the dark
Come lay beside me, let's ignite the spark

[Post-Chorus]

[Echo] (Bring your warmth to me)

[Verse 2]

[Chorus]

The midnight hour feels so bittersweet
Until the moment that our bodies meet
